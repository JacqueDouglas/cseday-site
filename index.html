CSE Day 2014

About

CSE Day is a day for all Computer Science, Computer Engineering, and anyone interested about CSE to come learn about the many opportunities they possess, be it in industry or in academia.

The day will be dedicated to a series of talks and panels where students are encouraged to ask whatever question pops into their mind about their CSE education. We also will be raffling company swag throughout the day and offering lunch and dinner!

The event will take place on Thursday, January 23, 2014 in CSE 1202. Hope to see you all there!
