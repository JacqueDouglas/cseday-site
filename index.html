CSE Day 2014

About

CSE Day is a day for all Computer Science, Computer Engineering, and anyone interested about CSE to come learn about the many opportunities they possess, be it in industry or in academia.

The day will be dedicated to a series of talks and panels where students are encouraged to ask whatever question pops into their mind about their CSE education. We also will be raffling company swag throughout the day and offering lunch and dinner!

The event will take place on Thursday, January 23, 2014 in CSE 1202. Hope to see you all there!

Directions and Parking for CSE day 2014

Address: Engineer Lane, University of California San Diego, San Diego, CA 92161

Location: Computer Science and Engineering Building, Room 1202 (First floor)

Parking directions: The name of the parking lot is either P503 or P502, you should see it on your right after on the Engineer Ln. As for which parking lot to park in, there are 10 reserved spaces in P502 and 4 reserved parking spaces in P503. Take either spot depending if it's being used. (Note: Look for the signs that says “CSE Day Reserved Parking” and follow direction to park your car at the location.)

Making your way to CSE: To find us go down the Engineer Ln but not to the end. About half-way through you will see a tiny alley on your left (which is next to a building). Go in and you will see a giant Bear statue on your right. The building across from you is where we are at.
