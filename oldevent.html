What's CSE Day?
CSE Day is day for all Computer Science, Computer Engineering, and anyone interested about CSE to come learn about the many opportunities they possess, be it in industry or in academia.

The day will be dedicated to a series of talks and panels where students are encouraged to ask whatever question pops into their mind about their CSE education.

The event will take place on Friday, May 4, 2012 in CSE 1202. Hope to see you there!

We'll have attendees from Facebook, Cisco, Northrop Grumman, Life Tech and more!
